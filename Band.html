The HCHS band is named The Pride because we perform with pride. We travel to different places for football games, concerts, contests, and festivals.

We have about one houndred and fourty-five band members. We consist of trumpets, clarinets, trumbones, baritones(Treble and Bass cleft), tubas, saxophones (tenor, alto, and baritone ), flutes, and piccalo.

Currently we are finishing up our marching season. Soon we will begin our concert season and work on more music as well as getting ready for concerts.
