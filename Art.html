Art 1

Art 1 mainly focuses on sketching and shading techniques, and how to draw with greater finesse and smoothness.

Art 2

Art 2 breaks down and explains the history of art and the many different style that have been used throughout the past.

Art 3

Art 3 is a mixture, and ranges anywhere from rough sketches to full-fledged paintings.

The Hardin County chapter of the National Art Honor Society is led by HCHS art teacher Hager Droke. There are annual meetings and get-togethers, and every year the whole group takes a trip to Memphis to view sculptures and other pieces of art.
